BEST SOCIAL ENTERPRISE CATEGORY

Taking Care of Business

TCB is a leading social enterprise dedicated to breaking the cycle of poverty through entrepreneurship and circular economy solutions. Since 2010, TCB has empowered thousands of South Africans through five core programmes: Resell, Repair, Remake, Reskill and Grow ECD. Our programmes successfully eradicate poverty and empower small businesses through our Heart-Head-Hands model of holistic human development. This includes life skills and resilience training, technical-, business- and financial skills training, mentorship and peer support. TCB offices in Cape Town, Durban, East London and Midrand support 2,000+ entrepreneurs annually. Our impact is independently measured by the Greenlight Poverty survey and ELOM studies.

Tswelangpele Co-operative Ltd

Tswelangpele Co‑operative Ltd is a pioneering social enterprise dedicated to simulation‑rich, compliance‑aligned curricula that empower communities through vocational dignity and future‑ready skills. Rooted in resilience and innovation, the company bridges design, compliance, and social entrepreneurship with climate action and ethical leadership. Its mission extends beyond training to holistic welfare, advocacy, and legacy protection. By integrating advanced technologies with accessible, layman‑friendly systems, Tswelangpele ensures inclusivity and impact across diverse audiences. The organization collaborates with government, corporates, and communities to build sustainable ecosystems, leaving a legacy of transformation, empowerment, and ethical growth across South Africa and the continent.

Career Build

CareerBuild is a registered NPO/PBO established in 2007 to raise a generation of informed, empowered South African youth. We operate high-impact skills development programmes, using a hands-on approach to bridge the gap between education and economic participation. Our innovative approach equips youth with essential job skills, entrepreneurial knowledge, and career plans based on their talents. Serving over 10,000 learners to date, CareerBuild directly tackles youth unemployment and promotes Social Equity in townships and rural communities.
